Our Charging Infrastructure

River


River is the more numerous yet slower of the Lightwave charger network. There are
currently 10s of thousands of Rivers 10s of thousands of Rivers in the world which
makes it possible for anyone to get to a full charge in 40 minutes. Rivers usually
come in pairs of two or three and they are free for anyone in any electric cart to
use.This is because it is Lightwaves ultimate goal to make buying an electric vehicle
of any kind as simple as possible for the average person
